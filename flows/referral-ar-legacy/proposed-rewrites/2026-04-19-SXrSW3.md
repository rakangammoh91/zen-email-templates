## Current live content

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Subject | لا تفوتي منتجاتك المجانية! شاركي مع أحباؤكِ كود خصمك اليوم🎁 |
| Preview | انشري متعة التألق بشعرٍ جميل واستمتعي معنا بمكافآت حصرية! |
| From | Zen Hair Shop · `info@zenhairshop.com` |

## Why it's underperforming

1. **Grammar error in subject** — "أحباؤكِ" is wrong; correct form is "أحبابكِ" or "من تحبّين". Arabic speakers clock this instantly and it suppresses trust.
2. **"كود خصمك" conceptually wrong** — the Referral flow is about **gifting friends a reward**, not about the user redeeming a discount code. This confuses the recipient about what action is being requested.
3. **"لا تفوتي" + emoji + exclamation stacking** — reads as tired urgency. This is email #5, the recipient has seen #1-4 already; the same urgency tone for the fifth time is noise.
4. **From-name is "Zen Hair Shop"** — inconsistent with Siham voice from rebuilt flows. Should be `سهام · زن هير`.

## Rewrite approach — use 2 of these 4; A/B the top 2

### Candidate 1 — Personal help-offer (RECOMMENDED)
```
Subject: صديقتك تستاهل · خلّيني أرسلّها بطاقة منّك بـ ١٥٠٠ ريال
Preview: آخر تذكير · الهدية جاهزة بانتظار اسمها.
```
First-person Siham. Specifies the prize (1500 SAR). Positions the recipient as generous, not opportunistic.

### Candidate 2 — Direct benefit · specific outcome
```
Subject: ١٥٠٠ ريال هدية لصديقتك · ومنتجات مجانية لكِ
Preview: مشاركة واحدة · طرفين مستفيدين · قبل لا تنتهي الحملة.
```
Clearer value exchange. Fixes the "كود خصمك" confusion.

### Candidate 3 — Curiosity
```
Subject: كم صديقة تستحق هدية منكِ؟
Preview: الإجابة بدقيقة · وكل واحدة بـ ١٥٠٠ ريال بطاقة هدايا.
```
Question opener. Reframes from "don't miss out" to "who deserves this".

### Candidate 4 — Social proof
```
Subject: نورة شاركت · حصلت صديقتها بطاقة + حصلت نورة منتج مجاني
Preview: نفس الفرصة لكِ · آخر يوم اليوم.
```
Named-customer pattern. Makes the mechanic concrete.

## Content recommendations (beyond subject)

- **Fix the from-name:** change to `سهام · زن هير` to match rebuilt-flow branding.
- **Remove the "discount code" framing** from body copy — it's a referral reward, not a discount.
- **Cadence check:** if email #5 fires <3 days after #4, push out to 5-7 days. Overlapping urgency kills opens on the final email.
- **CTA:** "شاركي الرابط" (share the link) not "استفيدي الآن" (benefit now).
- **Smart Sending: OFF**.

## Measurement plan

- Ship 2 variants (Candidate 1 + Candidate 2) as Klaviyo A/B.
- Sample size target: 200 recipients per variant over 21 days (Referral is lower volume).
- Declare winner if: open-rate ≥ 30% AND at least 3 referral events captured during test window.
