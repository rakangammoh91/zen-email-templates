## Current live content

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Subject | 🎁 فزتِ معنا بـ ١٥٠٠ ريال سعودي! استفيدي انت وصديقاتك الآن! |
| Preview | لحظات صداقة لا تُنسى مع بطاقة هدايا زن هير بقيمة ١٥٠٠ ريال سعودي. |
| From | Zen Hair Shop · `info@zenhairshop.com` |

## Why it's driving unsubs

1. **"فزتِ"** (you've won) is a false claim — she didn't win anything. Arabic-speaking Gulf audiences read "you've won" as a lottery scam pattern; it triggers the same suspicion as SMS prize-scams in Saudi.
2. **Starting a referral flow with a money number is transactional** — sets up the reader to see Zen Hair as "the brand that bribes me for shares" rather than "the brand that lets me gift my friends."
3. **Stacked exclamations + emoji + colloquial "استفيدي"** — combined cues read as spammy ad copy; high-value customers unsubscribe rather than flag, which is worse (quiet churn).
4. **"انت" should be "أنتِ"** — the kasra rule again.

## Rewrite approach — this is the entry email, so prioritize trust over clickbait

### Candidate 1 — Curiosity + gift framing (RECOMMENDED)
```
Subject: عندي فكرة تخلّي صديقتكِ تحبّكِ أكثر
Preview: بطاقة هدايا زن هير بـ ١٥٠٠ ريال · منّكِ لها.
```
Shifts the frame from "you won money" to "you're giving a gift". Matches the actual mechanic.

### Candidate 2 — Personal help-offer · Siham voice
```
Subject: اختارت شعرها · دورها تختار هديتكِ لصديقتها
Preview: خلّيني أجهّزلكِ الرابط · بطاقة ١٥٠٠ ريال جاهزة.
```
First-person Siham. Ties back to the customer's own purchase (she "chose her hair").

### Candidate 3 — Direct benefit · specific outcome
```
Subject: شاركي زن هير مع صديقة · بطاقة ١٥٠٠ ريال لها + منتج مجاني لكِ
Preview: مكافأة واضحة · بدون كود · بدون تعقيد.
```
Honest, specific. Removes the "win" framing entirely.

### Candidate 4 — Question opener
```
Subject: منو الصديقة اللي أول تتصلين بها وقت تلقين شي حلو؟
Preview: دور تلقين لها هدية · بطاقة ١٥٠٠ ريال من زن هير منّكِ.
```
Emotional anchor before the mechanic.

## Content recommendations (beyond subject)

- **Delete "فزتِ" from the body hero** — same root problem.
- **Fix the from-name:** `سهام · زن هير`.
- **Remove the promo code emphasis** from body — it's a gift card, not a coupon.
- **CTA:** "جهّزي الرابط" or "شاركي الآن" — avoid "استفيدي" (benefit/profit) as the action verb; it centers the recipient's gain, not her generosity.
- **Consider** softening the 1500 SAR headline in the body to "بطاقة هدية من اختيارك" and revealing the amount one scroll down — high-value numbers buried feel less transactional.

## Measurement plan

- Ship 2 variants (Candidate 1 + Candidate 3) as Klaviyo A/B.
- Sample size target: 200 recipients per variant over 21 days.
- Declare winner if: open-rate ≥ 35% AND **unsub rate ≤ 0.4%** (this is the critical metric — current is 0.694%).
- If unsub stays above 0.5% on both variants, the issue is not the subject — it's flow targeting. Audit the trigger segment.

## Priority

**Fix this before the other two briefs.** Email #1 underperformance compounds through the flow: every unsub here is a person who never sees emails #2-5. The RPR of the whole flow depends on this one.
