## Why it's underperforming

1. **Subject has no kasra** — "امرأة" is gender-neutral in effect; the Zen Hair voice rule (see `skills/zen-hair-arabic-voice`) requires feminine second-person address (`كِ` ending) to feel personal.
2. **Social-proof hero on email #2 is a mismatch** — she added to cart <24h ago; she wants a reason to finish her *own* order, not a testimonial. Social proof belongs on email #3 or #4 when friction is the real blocker.
3. **100,000 number is a credibility flag** — high-volume cart-abandon recipients read big round numbers as ad-copy; it pushes unsub.
4. **Preview repeats the same idea** — "آلاف النساء" + "جمالك" restates the subject instead of adding new information.

## Rewrite approach — use 3 of these 5; A/B the top 2

### Candidate 1 — Personal help-offer · Siham voice (RECOMMENDED)
```
Subject: خليني أساعدكِ تختارين · سلتك في انتظارك
Preview: أيش اللي مخليكِ مترددة؟ قوليلي وأرشحلكِ الأنسب لشعرك.
```
Removes social proof. Adds first-person Siham voice. Invites a reply.

### Candidate 2 — Direct benefit · specific outcome
```
Subject: سلتك فيها ما يخلّي شعرك يطول ٢٠ سم خلال أسبوع
Preview: قبل ما تنسين · هذي الطريقة اللي استخدمتها نورة وزادت طولها.
```
Numeric benefit. Named customer bridges to proof without being the proof.

### Candidate 3 — Curiosity · mirrors body hero
```
Subject: إيش اللي يفرّق بين إكستنشن يدوم شهر وواحد يدوم سنة؟
Preview: الجواب في السلة · راجعيها قبل لا تنتهي.
```
Question opener. Links curiosity to the cart content.

### Candidate 4 — Urgency · time-bound (ONLY if you run a cart-expiry feature)
```
Subject: سلتك تُحفظ ٤٨ ساعة بس · باقي ٢٤
Preview: لا تضيعين اللون اللي اخترتيه قبل يخلص من المخزون.
```
Don't ship this unless carts actually expire — false urgency costs trust.

### Candidate 5 — Social proof · curiosity (downgrade, not hero)
```
Subject: نورة اختارت نفس اللي في سلتك · هذي تجربتها
Preview: قبل لا تكملين طلبك · شوفي النتيجة بعد ٣٠ يوم.
```
Named single customer > anonymous big number. Keeps social proof but makes it specific.

## Content recommendations (beyond subject)

- **Cadence check:** confirm this email fires at 22-26h after add-to-cart. If <20h, push back — two emails in <24h drives the unsub.
- **CTA:** "أكملي طلبك" (finish your order) not "تسوقي الآن" (shop now).
- **Remove the 100k claim from the body hero** — move it to a sidebar or retire it on this email entirely.
- **Smart Sending: OFF** (matches rebuilt-flow policy; legacy was default ON — that's why mid-flow emails get suppressed).

## Measurement plan

- Ship 2 variants (Candidate 1 + Candidate 3) as Klaviyo A/B.
- Sample size target: 400 recipients per variant over 14 days.
- Declare winner if: open-rate gap ≥ 3pp AND unsub rate ≤ 0.3% AND RPR on this message ≥ prior 30d baseline.
- If both underperform vs. current (unlikely but possible), revert to current and rewrite from a different angle.
